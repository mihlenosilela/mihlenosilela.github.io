# Reflection: Mock Interview Experience

## Situation

The mock interview task required me to record a professional interview-style video as part of my WIL readiness. The purpose was to simulate a real interview environment and evaluate my communication and presentation skills.

## Task

My task was to confidently introduce myself, communicate my skills and experiences, respond to interview-style questions clearly, and present myself professionally within a limited time frame.

## Action

I prepared in advance by researching common interview questions and aligning my answers with my academic background and project experience.

To ensure a professional presentation, I dressed appropriately and selected a simple, distraction-free background. I recorded multiple takes of the video until I was satisfied with my delivery. During the recording, I focused on speaking clearly, maintaining eye contact with the camera, and presenting myself with confidence.

## Result

The mock interview significantly improved my confidence and communication skills. I became more aware of how I present myself and identified areas for improvement, such as pacing and clarity when speaking.

Overall, this experience has better prepared me for real-world interviews and reduced my anxiety about presenting myself in a professional setting.

## Challenges & Solutions

One of the main challenges I faced was feeling nervous while recording the video, which affected my confidence during the first few attempts. I overcame this by practicing my responses multiple times and recording several takes until I felt more comfortable and natural.

Another challenge was ensuring good video quality and clear audio. I addressed this by choosing a quiet environment and adjusting my positioning to improve lighting and visibility.

Additionally, I initially struggled with keeping my responses concise while still providing enough detail. I improved this by planning key points beforehand rather than memorising full answers, which helped me sound more natural and confident.
