## Reflection: Mock Interview Experience

## Situation
The mock interview task required me to record a professional interview-style
video to prepare for real workplace interviews as part of my WIL readiness.

## Task
My task was to confidently introduce myself, communicate my skills,
answer interview-style questions clearly, and present myself in a professional
manner within a limited time frame.

## Action
I prepared responses based on common interview questions and aligned them with
my academic background and projects. I dressed professionally, selected a plain
background, and recorded multiple takes until I was satisfied with my delivery.
I focused on clarity, confidence, and maintaining eye contact with the camera.

## Result
The mock interview significantly improved my confidence and communication skills.
I became more aware of my strengths and areas for improvement. This experience
has made me feel more prepared and less anxious about real job interviews.
